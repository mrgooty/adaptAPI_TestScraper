# Adapt Scraping Interview

Build a scraper that extracts structured data from these two mock insurance carrier sites:

- **Placeholder Carrier** — https://scraping-interview-website.onrender.com/placeholder_carrier
- **Forleast Star** — https://scraping-interview-website.onrender.com/forleast_star

They simulate real third-party carrier portals — similar data, different layouts. Use whichever language you're most comfortable with, and lean on coding agents freely. Treat the rendered site as your source of truth.

## What we're looking for

- **Completeness**: how much useful data about customers, policies, and agents you extract.
- **Robustness**: how your scraper handles missing fields, unexpected formats, and other surprises.
- **Solution quality**: how readable your code is, and how easy it would be to add a third carrier.

Time budget is 30–40 minutes. Judgment matters more than completeness — talk through tradeoffs as you go, and tell us what you'd do with more time.

## Output

A JSON array of the scraped data. Inputs to your scraper can be anything except the scraped data itself.
